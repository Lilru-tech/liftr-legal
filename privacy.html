Privacy Policy – Liftr Workout

Last updated: December 12, 2025

Liftr Workout ("the App") is developed and owned by David Gómez Sánchez.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address and username (required for authentication).
  • Optional profile data: avatar, sex, date of birth.
  • Workout data you voluntarily log in the app (workouts, exercises, scores, comments, etc.).
  • Diagnostics: basic technical data (e.g., crash logs) to improve stability and performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate users and maintain your account.
  • To store and display your workout history and related features.
  • To enable social features such as friends, rankings, and achievements.
  • To improve the app and fix bugs.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following providers:

  • Supabase – Database, Authentication, File storage: https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (optional) – Push notifications: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple Sign-In (optional) – Authentication: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

If the free version shows ads, advertising partners may process limited device data to serve ads, depending on your region and consent settings.

4. Your Rights / Account Deletion

You can delete your account from within the app (Settings → Delete account).
