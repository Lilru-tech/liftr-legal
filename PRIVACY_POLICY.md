# Privacy Policy – Liftr Workout

_Last updated: November 3, 2025_

Liftr Workout ("the App") is developed and owned by **David Gómez Sánchez**.

The App does not collect personal data outside of what is required to create and maintain a Supabase account (email, username, and optional profile data). All data is stored securely and processed only for the purpose of providing the app’s functionality.

## 1. Data We Collect
- Email address and username (required for authentication).
- Optional profile info: avatar, sex, date of birth.
- Workout data you voluntarily log in the app (exercises, scores, comments, etc.).

## 2. How We Use Your Data
- To authenticate users.
- To store and display your workout history.
- To enable social features such as friends, rankings, and achievements.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App uses the following providers:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Supabase | Database, Authentication, File storage | https://supabase.com/privacy |
| Firebase Cloud Messaging (optional) | Push notifications | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| Apple Sign-In (optional) | Authentication | https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ |

No data is sold or shared with advertisers.
